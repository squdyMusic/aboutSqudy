squdy

squdy is a beginner musician. this project is their way of learning how sound works — synthesis, mixing, structure, and building tracks from scratch. they don't use loops or presets. everything is made manually — from drum patterns to atmospheres.

for now, it's more of a demonstration of what they've learned through working on themselves. released so far: a mixtape pluto (2026), and the album kenoopsia is on its way. both projects are not about genre — they're about finding their own sound and learning to express a state of mind through music.

squdy is doing this to grow. nothing more.
